Design patterns collected from godly.website — curated animation and interaction references

Text Animation Patterns

Line-by-line text reveal
Each line of a heading reveals from behind a clipping mask, as if a shutter is opening. The text translates Y from 100% to 0% inside an overflow:hidden container. Very popular on Godly-featured agency sites.
TextHeroMask reveal
Scramble text effect
On hover or load, text scrambles through random characters before settling on the final word. GSAP's ScrambleText plugin or custom JS implementations. Popular for nav items, labels, and data-driven sites.
TextHoverInteractive
Number counting on scroll
Stats/metrics animate from 0 to their final value when they enter the viewport. Eased with power2 or power3. Common in "Our Results" sections. Makes data feel dynamic and earned.
StatsScrollCounter

Image & Media Patterns

Image reveal on scroll
An image is hidden behind a solid color block. On scroll, the block slides away (clip-path or transform) revealing the image underneath. Creates a dramatic presentation effect. Often used for portfolio thumbnails.
ImageScrollclip-path
Hover image follow
On a list of project items, hovering reveals a large preview image that follows the mouse. The image fades in/out with scale and opacity. Creates a sense of depth and interactivity in text-heavy lists.
ImageHoverCursor follow

Layout Patterns

Marquee logo row
Client logos or brand names scroll continuously in a ticker marquee. Often has a second row scrolling in reverse. Pause on hover. CSS animation or Magic UI's Marquee component. Signals social proof efficiently.
LayoutSocial proofContinuous
Bento grid features
Features/services presented in a grid of differently-sized cards (bento box style). Cards have varying heights and widths creating visual interest. Each card contains a mini-demo, icon, or illustration. Popularized by Apple and Linear.
LayoutFeaturesGrid
