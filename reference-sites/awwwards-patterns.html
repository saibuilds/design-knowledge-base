Awwwards Patterns

Recurring design patterns observed across Awwwards-winning sites. Use these as inspiration.

Navigation Patterns

Full-screen overlay menu
Hamburger icon triggers a full-screen dark overlay with large, staggered nav links that animate in from below. Often includes a footer row with social links. Closing plays reverse animation.
NavigationGSAP / FramerAgency
Floating pill navigation
A small pill-shaped nav that floats fixed at top center. Contains 3-5 links + a CTA button. Shrinks on scroll. Glassmorphism or solid dark background. Popularized by Linear, Vercel, and agency sites.
NavigationFramer MotionSaaS

Hero Patterns

Kinetic typography hero
Large display text fills most of the viewport. Words animate in one at a time or letter by letter using SplitText / custom split. Background is minimal (gradient, video, or solid dark). CTA appears after text sequence completes.
HeroGSAP SplitTextAgency
Full-bleed video hero
Autoplay muted video fills the viewport. Text overlaid with mix-blend-mode or semi-transparent overlay. Scroll arrow bounces at bottom. Often paired with a scroll-to-darken gradient overlay.
HeroVideoConstructionLuxury
3D object hero
Three.js or R3F 3D object (product, abstract shape, globe) rotates or reacts to mouse movement. Text is layered on top. The object typically goes from center on load to bottom-right on scroll (parallax depth shift).
HeroThree.js / R3FTech / Product

Scroll Patterns

Pinned horizontal scroll panel
A section pins in the viewport and horizontal panels scroll by as the user scrolls vertically. Used to present portfolio items, process steps, or case studies. GSAP ScrollTrigger with pin + horizontal xPercent tween.
ScrollGSAP ScrollTriggerPortfolio
Sticky sidebar + scrolling content
Left column (title/counter) sticks while right column scrolls through items. Each item triggers a state change in the left column (image swap, number increment). Used for case studies and process sections.
ScrollGSAP / IntersectionObserverAgency
Scrub parallax layers
Multiple image/element layers at different depths that move at different speeds as the user scrolls. Creates a cinematic 3D depth effect. Implemented with GSAP scrub or Framer's useTransform on multiple elements.
ScrollParallaxGSAP scrub

Interaction Patterns

Custom cursor with follower
Default cursor hidden. Custom dot follows mouse at native speed. Larger ring follows with spring lag. On hover over links/buttons: cursor enlarges, changes color, or shows a label ("View", "Drag"). GSAP quickSetter for performance.
CursorGSAPAgency
Page transition wipe
On link click: a colored panel wipes across the screen (top-to-bottom or left-to-right), then the new page wipes in from the other direction. Logo/nav element morphs during transition. Common colors: black, brand accent, white.
TransitionGSAP / FramerAgency
