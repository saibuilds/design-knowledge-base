Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Does applying affect my credit score? +

No. Our initial pre-qualification uses a soft credit pull that does not appear on your credit report and has zero impact on your score. A hard pull only occurs when you formally accept a mortgage offer.

How is ClearPath different from going to my bank? +

Your bank offers their own products only. We compare 50+ lenders simultaneously — including banks, credit unions, and monoline lenders — to find you the best rate for your specific situation. We're legally obligated to act in your interest, not the lender's.

What documents do I need to apply? +

To start: nothing. For a full pre-approval you'll need 2 years of T4s, 90 days of bank statements, a recent pay stub, and government ID. We'll guide you through exactly what's needed.

How much does ClearPath charge? +

Nothing. Our service is completely free for borrowers. We're compensated by the lender you choose. Legally, this cannot influence which lender we recommend — our fiduciary duty is to you.

Can I get a mortgage if I'm self-employed? +

Yes. We specialize in self-employed mortgages and have access to lenders with flexible stated-income programs. We've successfully placed hundreds of self-employed borrowers who were turned down by their banks.
