Legal Basement Apartment

Convert your existing basement into a fully legal, income-generating apartment. Fastest path to rental income with the lowest up-front cost of any suite type.

  • Full building permit & zoning compliance

Permit-to-Keys in 5 Steps

Step 01 — Week 1

Free Site Assessment

Our team visits your property to assess zoning eligibility, lot size, setbacks, and utility connections. We provide a detailed feasibility report within 48 hours.

Free — No Obligation
Step 02 — Weeks 2–4

Design & Permit Application

Our in-house architects draw full construction drawings. We submit the permit application to the City of Toronto and manage all correspondence.

Step 03 — Weeks 4–12

City Review & Permit Approval

The city reviews the application. We handle all revision requests and resubmissions. Our 100% permit approval rate means zero wasted time for you.
