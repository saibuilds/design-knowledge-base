How We Work

Our 6-Step Process

Every project follows the same proven framework — no surprises, no overruns, no excuses.

01
Planning & Scope
+

We start with a free in-home consultation to understand your vision, timeline, and budget. Our project manager prepares a full scope of work with itemized pricing — no vague estimates.

Free in-home consultation
Detailed written scope
Fixed-price contract (no surprises)
Timeline with milestones
02
Permit & Design
+

We handle every permit application with the City of Toronto. Our in-house designer produces 3D renders so you can visualise the finished space before any tools come out.

100% permit success rate
3D renders included
Material & finish selection
Engineering drawings if required
03
Demolition & Prep
+

Clean, careful demolition with full protection of existing finishes and daily debris removal. We leave your home as clean as we found it — every single day.

Daily clean-up protocol
Dust barriers and floor protection
Responsible waste disposal
Structural assessments
04
Build Phase
+

Framing, rough-ins, insulation, drywall, tile, cabinetry, flooring — executed by our own certified tradespeople. No sub-contracting. One team, one standard.

Own tradespeople (no sub-contracting)
Weekly progress photos
Dedicated site supervisor
Daily client updates via app
05
Finishing & Detailing
+

Trim, paint, fixtures, hardware — the details that separate good from great. Our finish team has an obsessive eye for quality. Punch list completed in full before handover.

100-point finishing checklist
Client walkthrough before sign-off
Professional cleaning included
Touch-up kit provided
06
Handover & Warranty
+

Final inspection, permit sign-off, keys in hand. We provide a 2-year workmanship warranty and 1-year follow-up support. Your satisfaction is our reputation.

City inspection & permit close-out
2-year workmanship warranty
Maintenance guide included
12-month check-in call
