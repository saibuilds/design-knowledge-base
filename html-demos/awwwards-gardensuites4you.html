How It Works

The 5-Step Permit Process

We handle every stage — from the initial feasibility check to the final occupancy permit. You provide the property, we handle the rest.

01
Step One
Free Consultation & Feasibility

We assess your property's zoning, lot coverage, setbacks, and servicing to confirm it qualifies for a suite. Most Toronto properties do — we'll tell you in 48 hours.

Free — 48 Hours
02
Step Two
Design & Engineering

Our architects and engineers produce full architectural drawings, structural calculations, and 3D renders. You'll know exactly what you're getting before permits are submitted.

3–4 Weeks
03
Step Three
City Permit Submission

We prepare and submit the complete permit package to the City of Toronto. Our 100% permit approval rate is the result of meticulous preparation — no re-submissions, no delays.

4–8 Weeks (City Processing)
04
Step Four
Build Phase

Site preparation, foundation, framing, rough-ins, insulation, exterior, and interior finishes — all completed by our in-house teams. Weekly progress updates with photos.

8–16 Weeks
05
Step Five
Final Inspection & Occupancy Permit

City inspection, occupancy permit issued, keys in hand. You're ready to rent. We also provide a full tenant-ready checklist and optional property management referral.
