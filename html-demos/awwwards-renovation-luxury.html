How We Work

Our Process

01
Discovery & Vision
We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your goals, lifestyle, and aesthetic vision. No detail is too small.
02
Design & Planning
Our design team creates detailed 3D renderings and full material specifications. You'll see your renovation before a single wall comes down.
03
Permits & Approvals
We handle all city permits, structural engineering sign-offs, and HOA approvals. Zero headache for you.
04
Expert Construction
Our dedicated trades team — carpenters, tilers, electricians, plumbers — execute with surgical precision on your timeline.
05
Final Reveal
A full walk-through with your project manager. We don't consider a job done until every last detail is perfect.
