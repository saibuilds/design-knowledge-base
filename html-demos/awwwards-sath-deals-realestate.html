Where We Work

Toronto's Finest Neighbourhoods

Deep local knowledge across the GTA's most sought-after communities — from historic Victorian streets to modern lakeside developments.

01
Rosedale

Toronto's most prestigious address. Stately homes, ravine lots, and an unmatched sense of historic elegance. Walking distance to Yonge & Bloor.

Avg. Home Price: $3.2M+
02
Forest Hill

Grand estates and lush canopy streets define this enclave. Home to UCC and BSS, with quiet cul-de-sacs and generous lot sizes.

Avg. Home Price: $2.8M+
03
The Annex

Vibrant and walkable. Victorian semis, indie cafés, and proximity to U of T make this a perennial favourite for families and investors.

Avg. Home Price: $1.4M+
04
Yorkville

Toronto's answer to the Upper East Side. Luxury condos, world-class dining, Hermès, Chanel, and Holt Renfrew at your doorstep.

Avg. Condo: $1.1M+
05
Leaside

Family-first community with top-ranked schools, Sunnybrook Park, and a charming village atmosphere. Among the fastest-appreciating markets in the GTA.

Avg. Home Price: $2.1M+
06
Mississauga

The GTA's second city. Waterfront communities, Port Credit, and Square One District offer exceptional value with transit access to downtown.

Avg. Home Price: $1.1M+
